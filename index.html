About the movies:

50 first dates

    This movie is a romantic comedy that tells the story of a man who falls in love with a woman who has short-term memory loss. It's a heartwarming and funny film that explores themes of love, commitment, and the importance of living in the moment.






The Amazing Spiderman

    This movie is a superhero film that follows the story of Peter Parker as he navigates his dual life as a high school student and the superhero Spiderman. It's an action-packed film with great special effects and a compelling storyline about responsibility and heroism.


Frankenstein

    This movie is a classic horror film that tells the story of a scientist who creates a living being from dead body parts. It's a thought-provoking film that explores themes of creation, responsibility, and the consequences of playing God.





The Notebook

    This movie is a romantic drama that tells the story of a young couple who fall in love during the early 1940s. It's a touching film that explores themes of love, memory, and the enduring power of relationships.





The Visit

    This movie is a horror-thriller that follows two siblings who visit their grandparents' remote farm, only to discover that something is very wrong. It's a suspenseful film with unexpected twists and turns that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats.
